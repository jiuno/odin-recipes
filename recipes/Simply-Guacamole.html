Simpy Guacamole

Description

This is so easy and so good. It's always the hit of the party and it's gone before anything else on the table.

Ingredients

  • 5 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • ¾ cup minced green onion
  • ½ cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and pepper, to taste

Steps

  1. Stir together the avocado and lemon juice ni a serving bowl.
  2. Add the green onion and cilantro.
  3. Mix well.
  4. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning.
